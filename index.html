What I do

Here are some aspects I highly value. For more information, see ToS.

Efficient, lag-free code.

I am an experienced Java developer with over two years of experience and several months of experience working with the Bukkit API. My code is reliable and efficient, and I am always seeking ways to improve my skills.

Configuartion

I greatly appreciate customizability, and offer a high level of it in my products. whether that is via the config or in-game commands. Messages, prefixes, and more, are all flexible.

Looks

I am committed to producing visually appealing and professional plugins that meet the highest standards of quality. Whether it is in-game graphics or the code itself, I strive to make every aspect of my plugins as polished and well-designed as possible.

Bug fixes

Before delivering any product, I thoroughly test it to ensure that it is of high quality. However, if you do encounter any bugs, I offer free bug fixes to the extent that it is reasonable.

Fast delivery.

You can expect to receive your plugin within 2-5 business days after payment, with some exceptions.

Terms of Service

These are some base rules I work with when creating plugins.

Payment

  • The price of the product is one we both agreed on before I start working. It may vary depending on the difficulty of the plugin, but generally, my prices are low.
  • You must pay at least half upfront. I don't start working before I receive at least half of the payment. The rest would be paid after I am done. You will only get the final product once you paid the full amount.
  • Adding new features counts as a different order. We will agree on exactly what the plugin does beforehand. I may ask for clarification afterwards.

Handing product policy

  • The product will be handed after 2 to 5 business days. If the request is complicated, it may take more time.
  • You are eligible for a refund after 4 weeks of not receiving the product.

Bug fixes

  • Bug fixes will be free for 2 weeks after the initial purchase.
  • You must provide clear instructions on how to reproduce the bug when submitting a bug report.
  • If the bug is caused by another plugin, it may not be possible to fix it.
  • Bug reports are submitted via Email or Discord. Just send a message!

Product distribution

  • You may not resell the product under any circumstances.
  • The source code of the product will not be shared with the public nor the customer.
